About us

PROVIDING WORLD CLASS

Products & Services

Sidmach Technologies is an emerging ICT giant in Africa, leading technological transformations that have affected over 100 million lives across various landscapes within the continent.


The company is renowned as a frontrunner in technology innovations that is rapidly changing lifestyle and business culture in Africa through its range of cutting edge solutions that have been deployed across different countries.


Founded in 1993 by five ICT experts, Adedayo P.S Arogundade, Hassan U. Alao, Chijioke A. Eke, Michael A. Olajide, and Samuel E. Udofia (Late), Sidmach has consistently led innovations in Africa for a quarter of a century.

We develop and support a range of innovative software solutions that improve the quality of work and life across the public and private sectors. For more than two decades, we have delivered scalable, proven, secure, and easy-to-use technologies with the ultimate goal of enhancing productive work and profitable businesses. We also proactively identify new areas of technology that can be leveraged for efficiency, productivity, and service delivery to customers.

At SIDMACH, we are passionate about the endless transformative possibilities of Information Technology, and the limitless opportunities ICT offers across every facet of life and business.
